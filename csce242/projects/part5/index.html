About This Project

This project is the culmination of multiple iterations and valuable input from instructors, TAs, and peers. Our goal was to create an intuitive, accessible, and professional platform that effectively communicates our purpose.

We have utilized HTML, CSS, and media queries to ensure that everything scales well across different devices, from desktop to mobile.

The final look follows a clean and consistent design approach, complete with a fully functional hamburger menu on smaller screens. No text runs offscreen, and no images stretch unproportionally.
